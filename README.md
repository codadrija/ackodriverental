This repository contains the proposal and prototype for adding a **P2P Car Rental Marketplace** feature to [Acko Drive](https://www.ackodrive.com/).

## 🎯 Problem Statement
Currently, Acko Drive allows users to **buy cars online** but does not support rentals. Adding a **peer-to-peer car rental system** would:
- Generate extra income for car owners.  
- Give renters access to more variety.  
- Keep users engaged even after purchase.  
- Seamlessly integrate with Acko’s insurance services.  

## 💡 Proposed Solution
A platform similar to **Airbnb but for cars**, where:
1. **Owners** list their cars with details and availability.  
2. **Renters** search, book, and pay through Acko Drive.  
3. **Acko** provides short-term insurance and takes a service fee.  
4. **Trust & Safety** ensured via verification, condition checks, and reviews.  

## ⚙️ Workflow
1. Owner Listing → Car profile + price set  
2. Renter Search & Booking → Filters + instant/approval-based booking  
3. Payment & Insurance → In-app payment + short-term insurance  
4. Handover & Return → Meet-up or doorstep + checklist  
5. Ratings & Reviews → Both parties review each other  

## 🚧 Challenges
- Verification of users and documents.  
- Liability & insurance for damages.  
- Building trust for initial adoption.
